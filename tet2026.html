Tet 2026 Program

Strategic Customer Experience Campaign

Program Overview

The Tet 2026 Program is one of the most important customer engagement campaigns of the year. The initiative focuses on enhancing customer loyalty, increasing festive sales performance, and strengthening emotional connections with VIP customers.

Objectives

• Increase festive season revenue
• Improve VIP customer retention
• Deliver premium omnichannel experiences
• Strengthen PNJ brand perception

Key Activities

• Personalized Tet gifts
• VIP customer events
• Exclusive member promotions
• CRM & digital communication campaigns
• In-store festive decoration activation
